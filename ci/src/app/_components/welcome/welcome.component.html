Introduction
Once you log in, you will see all topics, organized into units and levels. Each topic contains a sequence of lessons. To complete a lesson, you need to correctly answer several questions (this number can be changed in your settings). There are different ways to advance through the material. You can take a placement test and skip some topics. Or, once inside a topic, you can test out of it and move to the next topic. You can always go back to any topic or any lesson you have completed, to review it.
